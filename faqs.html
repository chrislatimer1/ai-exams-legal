Cloud Exams FAQs

How many exams and questions are included in Cloud Exams?

Cloud Exams covers 30 exams, each containing 400 questions. This provides you with a total of 12,000 unique cloud exam questions.

When are the questions updated?

The questions are refreshed each year to reflect the updated syllabuses of the exams.
